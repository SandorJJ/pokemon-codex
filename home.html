General Information

Currently, there are a total of nine generations Pokemon and each generation has multiple core games:

  1. Red and Green, Blue, Red and Blue, Yellow
  2. Gold and Silver, Crystal
  3. Ruby and Sapphire, FireRed and LeafGreen, Emerald
  4. Diamond and Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold and SoulSilver
  5. Black and White, Black 2 and White 2
  6. X and Y, Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire
  7. Sun and Moon, Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, Let's Go Pikachu! and Let's Go Eevee!
  8. Sword and Shield, Sword and Shield Expansion Pass, Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl, Legends: Arceues
  9. Scarlet and Violet, The Hidden Treassure of Area Zero
